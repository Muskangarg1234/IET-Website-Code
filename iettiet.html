IET Student Chapter

IET Student Chapter,TIET is a technical society which is a chapter of Institute of Engineering and Technology(IET),London.

IET Student Chapter aims at supporting the students to explore their professional and intellectural abilities,to develop their skills,increasing their competance, adopting the right attitude to maximize their potential and building a professional network that will help them to succeed in their professional endeavors.

EVENTS

Alumini Talk by Mrs.Sonia Madan(GM-EIL)[13 March 2020]

In an Alumni talk conducted by IET Student Chapter, Mrs Sonia Madan, General Manager of Engineers India Limited, She enlighted the various skills a student should acquire to get placed in core companies. The event started with a welcome address by Ms Manbir Kaur, President of IET Student Chapter TIET.



NSIC Rajpura Workshop[11 April 2019]

The workshop conducted at National Small Industries Corporation (NSIC), Rajpura provided students with hands on experience on some working projects. Students gained practical knowledge on various protection schemes used in domestic appliances, knowledge of Quality Control of electrical applications and testing of electrical appliances as per IEEE standards.
